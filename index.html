"Zeke's Peak" / "Ice Cold Beer"

A proof-of-concept game based on an arcade game from the 80's.

Gameplay:

  • Move the bumpers up & down to control the bar, and guide the ball on the bar past the losing holes (red) towards the winning hold (green).

Controls:

  • Left-bumper: Q (up) / D (down)
  • Right-bumper: P (up) / K (down)

Note:

  • The gameplay is unfinished, so win or lose you'll have to refresh the page to restart.
